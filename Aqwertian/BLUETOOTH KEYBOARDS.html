Using a Bluetooth Keyboard


Aqwertyan lets you use your bluetooth iPad keyboard to play! Simply connect your keyboard, and launch the app. If your keyboard does not seem to be working, try navigating to another screen (such as the Library), and then returning to the Performance screen. This will reset the external keyboard connection. If you still have problems, verify that the keyboard is working by typing some text. You can bring up an email form for testing by tapping the "Feature/Request" button at the bottom of this screen.


When using a bluetooth keyboard, all notes will automatically be held for the correct length of time. This is because there is no way to detect key-up events, so Aqwertyan cannot know how long you hold the note for.
