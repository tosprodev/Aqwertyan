Using External MIDI Instruments & Devices


Using your external MIDI device or instrument is easy! Simply connect your device to your iPad, and you will see one or two MIDI symbols light up on the Performance screen (look in the bottom left corner - one is for input, and the other is for output). Once the input symbol is lit up, you're good to go! Start playing your instrument to perform the music on the screen. Any note you play will be translated to the correct note in the song.
